Peak window

Recommendation: schedule the hardest tasks here.

Afternoon drift

Distractions rise after 3:00 PM while completion slows.

Recommendation: use shorter sprints + planned breaks.

Context switching cost

Even a 12% switching share can lower perceived efficiency.

Recommendation: batch admin tasks into a single block.

Read Reflection

Building this dashboard made me think about how easily “a productive day” can be reduced to numbers. Metrics like focus hours, completion rate, and efficiency score are useful for comparison, but they also simplify complex experiences into clean signals that platforms can rank and optimize. FOCUSLY is designed to show how dashboard logic can shape what we notice, what we value, and what we try to improve.
